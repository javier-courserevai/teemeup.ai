Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide reporting information. teemeup.ai uses cookies and similar technologies (such as local storage and session storage) to operate and improve the Service.

2. Types of cookies we use

Category Purpose Examples Duration
Essential Required for the Service to function. These cannot be disabled. Session authentication, CSRF protection, load balancing Session / up to 30 days
Functional Remember your preferences and settings to improve your experience. Language preference, last searched location, UI theme Up to 12 months
Analytics Help us understand how visitors use the Service so we can improve it. Data is aggregated and anonymised. Page views, feature usage, session duration (via privacy-focused analytics) Up to 13 months
Marketing Used to measure the effectiveness of our campaigns. Only set with your consent. Conversion tracking from ads, referral attribution Up to 90 days

3. Third-party cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services that appear on our pages. We have no direct control over these cookies. Current third-party integrations that may set cookies include:

  • Google Fonts — serves web fonts. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Unsplash — serves course imagery. Subject to Unsplash's Privacy Policy.
  • Booking partners (GolfNow, Chronogolf, TeeOff) — may set cookies when you initiate a booking redirect.

4. Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent parts of the Service from functioning correctly. Links to cookie management guides for common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

5. Your consent

When you first visit teemeup.ai, we will ask for your consent to set non-essential cookies via a cookie banner. You may withdraw consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or contacting us at hello@teemeup.ai.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology or regulation. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last revised. Continued use of the Service following any update constitutes acceptance.

7. Contact

Questions about our use of cookies? Email us at hello@teemeup.ai.
